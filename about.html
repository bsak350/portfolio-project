About Brittany

Healthcare & Product Management

Brittany has almost 10 years of experience in healthcare. She has extensive experience in medical devices working for Medtronic Diabetes as an Inside Sales Representative, Medtronic Cardiac Rhythm Management as a Clinical Specialist, and Curonix (formerly Stimwave) as a Clinical Specialist. She also has experience in healthcare IT working for Orion Health as a Product Manager.

Web & Application Development

Brittany has experience in web and application development. She enrolled in the SheCodes Professional Program: SheCodes Basics, SheCodes Plus, SheCodes React, and SheCodes Responsive and received certifications in Front-End Development, React Development, and Responsive Development. Additionally, through her time as a consultant developer at EPI-USE, she has utilizing the Mendix low-code rapid application development platform to build web-based and native mobile applications. She also has specialized training in Agile and Scrum methodologies, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Visual Studio Code Editor, API, GitHub, Bootstrap, Hosting via Netlify, and Slack.

Social Media & Digital Marketing

Additionally, Brittany has dual-certifications from the American Marketing Assocation and Digital Marketing Institute as a Digital Marketer and Certified Digital Marketing Professional. The course concluded with a proctured exam to ensure proficiencies in Content Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Paid Search (PPC) Using Google Ads, Email Marketing, Website Optimization, Google Analytics, Digital Marketing Strategy, and Display and Video Advertising. She has utilized this training to work as a freelance Social Media Marketing Consultant at Martinesque, LLC. and BSAK Media to find social media influencers for brand deals with for small independent brands.
